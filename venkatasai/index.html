Welcome to Venkata Sai Caterers & Events

We serve tasty Food for Every Occasion in Tirupati, Vijayawada, Hyderabad (any location in AP & TS).

Venkata Sai Catering provides fresh, tasty, and good-quality food for all kinds of functions like weddings, parties, family events, and office programs.

We prepare both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes, and you can choose your own menu based on what you like. All our food is made with natural and high-quality ingredients, checked and approved by government food inspectors for safety and cleanliness.

Our experienced team cooks with love and serves with a smile. We also help you plan the menu, decoration, and service to make your event more special and enjoyable.

We serve from 100 people to any number of guests, based on your need. You will get great taste, best quality, and good prices all in one place.

Venkata Sai Caterers not only serve food We serve happiness to you and your guests!
